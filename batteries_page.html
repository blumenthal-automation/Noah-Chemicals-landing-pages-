Battery Procurement Validation Requirements

What purity thresholds are strictly enforced for VRFB liquid electrolytes?

To eliminate capacity decay via cross-contamination in Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries (VRFB), we strictly enforce purity levels exceeding 99.9% on precursor V2O5, leveraging ICP-OES validation to lock out trace iron and silica.

Can you accommodate established proprietary formulas for battery technologies?

Yes. Through our Custom Chemical Synthesis and Toll Blending infrastructure, we regularly execute proprietary electrolyte and catalyst formulas under strict, legally binding Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs).

How do trace metal impurities affect NMC cathode longevity?

Even parts-per-million (ppm) levels of trace transition metals like Iron or Copper in precursor salts can instigate voltage suppression and trigger parasitic side-reactions, rapidly degrading cycle life. We resolve this by strictly isolating our synthesis lines to guarantee absolute compliance.

What is the MOQ for custom LTO or VRFB precursors?

Because we operate utilizing a vast global distribution network anchored by our domestic facility, we possess extreme agility. We supply small quantities for initial bench tests and logically scale immediately to metric tons as grid infrastructure deploys.
