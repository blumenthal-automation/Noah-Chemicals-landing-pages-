Nuclear Supply Chain Validations

How does Noah Chemicals verify the purity of advanced salts like Lithium Fluoride?

We operate an extensive in-house analytical laboratory utilizing Inductively Coupled Plasma Optical Emission Spectroscopy (ICP-OES) to guarantee Parts-Per-Billion (PPB) trace metal tolerances, which is mandatory for nuclear applications to prevent parasitic neutron capture.

What is the minimum order quantity for custom nuclear compounds?

We specialize in rapid scalability. We routinely supply extremely pure 50-gram batches for bench-scale SMR testing, scaling directly up to continuous multi-ton palletized deliveries for active deployment.
